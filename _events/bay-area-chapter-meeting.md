**Register @ [bit.ly/twc-bay-meeting](https://bit.ly/twc-bay-meeting)**

Our next chapter-wide general meeting will be on Zoom on Thursday, April 2. All new and current members are welcome, whether this is your first TWC Bay Area event or you're active in a working group. We'll introduce the structure of the chapter, hear updates from working groups, and talk through the results of the [member skills and interest survey](https://cosmic-knight-d0e.notion.site/31176edfa0728000aa27d6d1d8be1c3f). 

We'll also start planning for May Day 2026! Last year we [formed a contingent](https://techworkerscoalition.org/bay-area/) with partner tech worker organizations Alphabet Workers Union and Bay Area Labor 4 Palestine, and we're planning to build on that this year. We'll start planning for an art build and outreach to our comrades and coworkers to march together on May Day.
