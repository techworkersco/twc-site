Join us for June’s Book Club! In honor of Juneteenth, we'll read "[Legendary Queen of Fillmore](https://sfbayview.com/2015/02/legendary-queen-of-fillmore-leola-king-leaves-proud-legacy-of-struggle-against-redevelopment/)", an article on the gentrification of the Fillmore district, a historically Black neighborhood in SF. And in honor of Pride month, we'll also be reading an [article on the 2017 SF "Queers Hate Techies" exhibit](https://missionlocal.org/2017/06/queers-hate-techies-gay-shames-controversial-ata-exhibit). (A supplementary reading, a longer article about the [organization Gay Shame](https://thenewinquiry.com/virus-in-the-system/), is recommended but optional.) 

We hope to see you there for a fruitful discussion!
