This month's reading is White Supremacy Culture by Tema Okun. This reading examines the tools of white supremacy that run our lives, relationships, workplaces, and organizations. We'll look at how these tools coincide with the tools of capitalism and reflect on what it actually takes to unlearn them and live the antidotes instead.
