# Tech Workers Coalition Bay Area

We are the Bay Area chapter of TWC. We are building worker power in the Bay Area tech industry to improve our working conditions, and to have a bigger say in how the technology we build impacts our communities and the world.

We build power primarily through community building, political education, and training to organize and take collective action effectively as workers. We regularly gather in-person in San Francisco and the East Bay, and digitally on Zoom. As of Spring 2026 we're also starting to be active in South Bay again!

## About

- We define ourselves broadly as workers in and around the tech industry, labor organizers, community organizers, and friends. If this sounds like you, you're welcome here!
- We host regular social meetups, reading groups, chapter meetings, and political education events (often in partnership with aligned orgs). See the [calendar below](#upcoming-events) for upcoming events.
- We have active working groups focused on politicizing and educating fellow tech workers, pushing back against militarism and mass surveillance in tech, activating members, and organizing in our workplaces.
<br/><br/>

## Connect With Us

We're excited to connect with new members! When you [join TWC](/subscribe/), select the "SF Bay Area" chapter to be added to our local mailing list. Then, come introduce yourself in `#local-bay-area` Slack channel, share your ideas and learn about (or plan!) local events. We also periodically share upcoming local events in our chapter email newsletter.
